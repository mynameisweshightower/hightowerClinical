About

Hightower Clinical, LLC

Participating in clinical research can be a complicated and tedious process for physicians.

It is our mission to provide physicians with the resources that will enable them to offer newer and better treatments to their patients through clinical trials.

We fulfill this mission by taking the administrative burden off of the physician so they are able to offer the highest quality of care possible to their patients. By collaborating with physicians, sponsors and CROs, we are able to ensure that clinical research can touch the lives of as many people as possible.
