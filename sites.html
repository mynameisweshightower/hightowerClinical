Sites

Our research personnel are ACRP certified clinical research coordinators and have current CITI ICH/GCP training.

We prefer Western IRB but are open to a central IRB of the sponsor's choosing.

Our sites are all equipped with the following research-specific items in addition to the sites clinical equipment:

  • Locking -20 °C freezer
  • Locking refrigeration
  • Temperature monitoring for all study products
  • Limited access storage
  • Ambient centrifuge
  • ECG
  • Ample monitoring space including internet, phone, fax, and copier
